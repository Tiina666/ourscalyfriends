SNAKES


LISBET

Lisbet is an amelanistic corn snake. She has red eyes and she's a very curious creature. She's always ready for a romantic dinner with a mouse or two.

NERGAL

Nergie here is a little escape artist. He came from a local animal shelter after slithering away from his previous owner. He's very greedy and always searching for a mouse to eat.

BETTY

Named after the iconic animal lover and overall a great person, Betty White.
Our Betty is a blue-eyed leucistic ball python and she's very shy and sweet. Sometimes she's afraid of her own food.
